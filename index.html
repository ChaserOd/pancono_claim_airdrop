What is different between Pancono coins and Points?

At Pancono.Core.Wallet.zip folder users will be able to mine Pancono Coins. Whereas at Core.wallet.zip file users will be able to mine Points of other projects of out partners who have launched their own coin at Pancono ecosystem. When you mine Pancono coins, you are directly participating in the Pancono airdrop, and all rewards will be credited in Pancono coins (PANNO). When you mine Points, it means you are mining other newly launched coins of partner's projects that will be added to the Pancono ecosystem during the Mainnet launch.
