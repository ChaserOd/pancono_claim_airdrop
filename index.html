Pancono Wallet & Blockchain Features

  • Full-node architecture similar to Bitcoin Core
  • UTXO-based transaction model
  • Proof-of-Work consensus
  • SHA-256 cryptography
  • Peer-to-peer networking
  • LevelDB blockchain storage
  • Secure transaction signing

Total Supply

Pancono has a fixed maximum supply of 30 million coins. Limited supply increases long-term scarcity and rewards early miners and community members.
